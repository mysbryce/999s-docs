## Usage

<CodeGroup>
```lua server-or-client/your-file.lua
local tasks = {}

table.insert(tasks, function(cb)
    print('do task')
    cb()
end)

Async.parallel(tasks, function(results)
    print('Tasks completed one task')
end)
```
